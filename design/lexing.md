# Derivative Rules & Calculator Definitions

$$
\begin{align*}
num \to Const(num) \\
var \to Var() \\
\verb|^|num \to Exp(num) \\
\end{align*}
$$

Constant Factor Rule: $$(af)' = a * f' \to ConstFact(a, f)$$

Sum Rule: $$(f + g)' = f' + g' \to Sum(f, g)$$

Difference Rule: $$(f - g)' = f' - g' \to Diff(f, g)$$
